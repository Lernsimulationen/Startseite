# Suno-Prompts für die Tafelansicht

Die Musik soll die Deutschlandreise unterstützen, aber Sprache, Fragen und Antworten nicht überdecken. Für alle Varianten in Suno am besten **Instrumental**, **keine Vocals**, **kein gesprochenes Wort**, **kein Jingle**, **keine bekannten Künstlernamen** und einen sauberen Loop-fähigen Anfang/Schluss verwenden.

## 1. Hauptmotiv – Aufbruch

```text
Instrumental cinematic travel soundtrack for an educational quiz game shown on a classroom screen. A modern evening train journey through Germany: hopeful forward motion, elegant piano ostinato, warm strings, restrained electronic pulse, subtle rail-like rhythm, soft brass swells, deep night-blue atmosphere with golden lights. Energetic but focused, intelligent and contemporary, suitable as quiet background music while students read questions. No vocals, no spoken words, no lyrics, no announcer, no sudden drops, no aggressive drums, no quotation of existing songs. Seamless 90-second loop, polished soundtrack production.
```

Dateiname: `assets/audio/tafelfahrt-main.mp3`

## 2. Ruhig – Denkpause

```text
Instrumental ambient study music for a social-science classroom quiz about a train journey through Germany. Calm reflective evening mood, soft electric piano, gentle marimba accents, airy synth pads, subtle brushed percussion and distant warm strings. Spacious, clear and unobtrusive so students can concentrate on difficult questions. Modern editorial travel mood, quiet curiosity, no vocals, no spoken words, no lyrics, no suspense horror, no heavy bass, no sudden transitions. Seamless 90-second loop, clean and understated.
```

Dateiname: `assets/audio/tafelfahrt-calm.mp3`

## 3. Finale – Gipfelmoment

```text
Instrumental uplifting finale music for an educational team quiz reaching the Zugspitze. Begin with focused pulse and restrained tension, then build into a hopeful mountain-top climax with cinematic strings, warm brass, piano, light taiko-style percussion and shimmering sky-blue synth texture. Triumphant but tasteful, suitable under a final question and score reveal, never overpowering speech. No vocals, no spoken words, no lyrics, no famous melody, no trailer bombast, no harsh distortion. 90-second arc with a clean loopable ending.
```

Dateiname: `assets/audio/tafelfahrt-finale.mp3`

## Einbau

Die MP3-Dateien einfach mit den oben genannten Namen in `assets/audio/` legen. Die Tafelansicht bietet anschließend automatisch Auswahl, Play/Pause und Lautstärke.
